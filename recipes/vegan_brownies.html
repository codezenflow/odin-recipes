Vegan Brownies

Home

Description

These rich and fudgy vegan brownies are easy to make and irresistibly delicious—perfect for satisfying any chocolate craving.
Made with simple pantry staples and no dairy or eggs, they come together in just one bowl.
Whether you're vegan or not, these brownies are sure to be a crowd-pleaser!


Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups granulated white sugar
  • ¾ cup good-quality unsweetened cocoa powder, sifted
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ¾ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup hot water or coffee
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish with baking spray; line bottom with parchment paper.
  2. Whisk flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt together in a large bowl until combined.
  3. Pour in water (or coffee), vegetable oil, and vanilla; mix until well-blended. Spread batter evenly in the prepared dish.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until top is no longer shiny and center feels just set when gently pressed, about 30 minutes.
  5. Let cool for at least 10 minutes before cutting into 15 squares.
